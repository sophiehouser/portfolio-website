GIFS

Photoshop, Painting & Drawing

Memes, just a photo with a few simple words or a short video, are often able to translate much deeper, larger feelings, sometimes things so relatable that would fall flat in another medium. I’m interested in the way very small pieces of information can express a mood, share a feeling or tell a story. I’m especially interested in GIFs and short continuous loops. It’s exciting to see my physical drawings, paintings and collages brought to life using digital animation.

Large Scale Visuals

Night time visuals for the main stage and square of The Palace Residency

In 2019, I spent 10 days at The Palace Residency, collaborating and working on projects with 40 other international artists at a castle in Poland. I handled visuals most nights in the main square and during live performances on the main stage there. I projected reels of my GIFs onto the facade of the palace wall.

A Modern Lady

From her genesis to her inner mind, who IS she?

She’s a modern lady: born into the burning heap of late capitalism, the internet age, and the resurgence of Y2K aesthetics. This is a look at her birth, the inside of her mind and her late night, moon lit searches. May Google’s ranking algorithm, or otherwise Ottolenghi's Simple Cookbook, save her.
