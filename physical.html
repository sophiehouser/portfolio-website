GRAPES EATING GRAPES EATING GRAPES

Conception & Painting

This is a world of bread and circuses. Grapes lust after grapes, too caught up by the fruit to see the hands above orchestrating, holding the bunches just far enough a part. Behind them, another larger grape bunch comes to feed as well, and behind them probably lies an even larger bunch. We are all grapes on a vine, distracted by what we want, unable to see the danger behind us, or the danger above.

THE SQUARE BRA

Conception, Contextual Research & Creation

Breasts come in all shapes and sizes, yet the normal breast shape is round and perky. Bras mold breasts into this shape and create a false standard. In the 50s, for example, the bullet bra was popular and a bullet shaped breast was the norm. To show the way bras mask true breast shape and create a standard, I imagined a world where the normal bra shape was a square. In that world, a square breast would be normal. I 3D printed the cups and sewed the straps, then I wore my bra for the day.
