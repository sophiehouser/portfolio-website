GRAPES EATING GRAPES EATING GRAPES

Conception & Painting

This is a world of bread and circuses. Grapes lust after grapes, too caught up by the fruit to see the hands above orchestrating, holding the bunches just far enough a part. Behind them, another larger grape bunch comes to feed as well, and behind them probably lies an even larger bunch. We are all grapes on a vine, distracted by what we want, unable to see the danger behind us, or the danger above.
